Sushi

Sushi is a Japanese dish consisting of vinegared rice combined with ingredients like raw or cooked seafood, vegetables, and seaweed..

Burger

A sandwich made from ground meat, usually Chicken, placed inside a bun.

Red pasta

Red pasta is a dish with tomato-based sauce, including ingredients like tomatoes, garlic, onions, and herbs, commonly served with pasta.

White Pasta

"White pasta" typically refers to pasta made from refined wheat flour, devoid of the bran and germ, resulting in a lighter-colored product.

Pizza

A popular dish made of a flat round base of dough topped with cheese, tomatoes, and various other ingredients.

Chicken lollipop

Chicken lollipop is a popular appetizer made from chicken wings or drumettes, seasoned, battered, and deep-fried to resemble lollipops.
